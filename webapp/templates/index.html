About Us

Pun Space is a new coworking space in Chiang Mai (opening in March 2013), located 2 minutes from Nimman area. "Pun" in Thai (ปัน) means "share". So Pun Space simply translates to "Shared Space".

We are open Monday to Saturday from 9am to 6pm for non-members (i.e. day pass and flexi holders).

We are open 24/7 for members.

Pun Space is a fun, friendly and collaborative workspace. It’s not just a shared office but a place where you can meet new friends and business partners, where you can work comfortably without overstaying your welcome like working in a coffee shop, a place where you can leave your laptop out without worrying. Come come check out our space and become a part of our community.
