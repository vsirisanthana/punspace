About Us

Pun Space is a new coworking space in Chiang Mai (opening in March 2013), located 2 minutes from Nimman area. "Pun" in Thai (ปัน) means "share". So Pun Space simply translates to "Shared Space".

Pun Space is a fun, friendly, and collaborative workspace. It’s not just a shared office but a place where you can meet great new people with mutual interest, a place where you can work comfortably for long hours without feeling overstay your welcome like working in a coffee shop, and a place where you can leave your laptop to go to bathroom without fear. Come be part of our community.
